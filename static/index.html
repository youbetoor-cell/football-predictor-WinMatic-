Feature-rich match modelling
Team indices, attack/defence strength, xG-style proxies, form, rest days and bookmaker implied probabilities are all fed into the model to estimate home/draw/away goal distributions.

Calibrated 1X2 probabilities

Every fixture gets fair home / draw / away probabilities, tuned on past seasons instead of blindly trusting odds.

Edge vs closing prices

WinMatic compares its probabilities to live market odds and flags spots where model value is significantly higher.

Full audit trail

Predictions, edges and results are stored so you can track hit rates, ROI and model drift over time.
